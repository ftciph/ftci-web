About FTCI

Who We Are

FANATXT TEXTERS CLUB INC. (FTCI) is a nonprofit youth-serving organization in the Philippines dedicated to creating positive change in communities through leadership, volunteerism, and environmental stewardship.

Founded on the principle that every young person has the power to make a difference, FTCI empowers Filipino youth to become agents of change in their communities and the environment.

  • Youth Leadership Programs
  • Environmental Conservation Initiatives
  • Community Outreach Programs

Our Mission

To empower Filipino youth and communities through volunteerism, leadership development, environmental initiatives, and social responsibility programs that create lasting positive impact.

Our Vision

A united and empowered community of responsible citizens working together for a better, more sustainable Philippines where every young person can reach their full potential.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to FTCI?

You can donate through PayPal (https://paypal.me/ftci2006?country.x=PH&locale.x=en_US) or GCash (09272278612). All donations are transparent and directly support our programs.

Can I volunteer part-time?

Yes! We offer flexible volunteer opportunities. Whether you can commit a few hours weekly or participate in special events, there's a role for you in FTCI.

What age can you volunteer?

You must be at least 16 years old to volunteer with FTCI. We welcome young people from all backgrounds who are passionate about community service.

How is my donation used?

100% of donations support our youth empowerment programs, environmental initiatives, and community outreach. We maintain full transparency in our financial reporting.
