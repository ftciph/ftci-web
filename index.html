About FTCI

FANATXT TEXTERS CLUB INC. (FTCI) is a nonprofit youth-serving organization in the Philippines dedicated to leadership, volunteerism, environmental protection, and community service for over 20 years.

Our Mission

To empower Filipino youth and communities through volunteerism, leadership development, environmental initiatives, outreach programs, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

A united and empowered generation of responsible citizens working together for a better Philippines through compassion, leadership, and community service.
