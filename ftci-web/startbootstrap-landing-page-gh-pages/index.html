About FTCI

FANATXT TEXTERS CLUB INC. (FTCI) is a nonprofit youth-serving organization in the Philippines dedicated to leadership, volunteerism, environmental protection, and community service.

Our Mission

To empower Filipino youth and communities through volunteerism, leadership development, environmental initiatives, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

A united and empowered community of responsible citizens working together for a better Philippines.
